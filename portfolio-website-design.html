Building my Portfolio Website

Project Overview

The aim of this project was to build myself a portfolio website from scratch and to learn about frontend development in the process.

My site would need to work on a practical level, functioning across devices and providing viewers with quick access to the information about me and my work they would need. It would also need to work on an emotional level, engaging viewers and encouraging curiosity.

After researching ideas, I began to put together the design in HTML and CSS.

The build was an iterative process of adjustments focussed on achieving the balance between functionality, a visually professional site and showing personality.
