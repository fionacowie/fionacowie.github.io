ABOUT ME

My journey into product design

I've journeyed into product design from a background in jewellery design, retail display and running a side business creating and selling my illustrations. Drawing on these experiences, I take a holistic approach that blends aesthetics, design psychology, and strategic thinking. This helps me create designs that balance user needs with business goals, while also honing in on the details that elevate a product and spark joy.

Let's work together!

I love bringing ideas to life on projects that have a positive impact on people's lives and the world around us. From crafting details that make you smile to collaborating on game-changing products, I believe in design that makes a difference.

I'd love to connect and explore ideas together ✨
